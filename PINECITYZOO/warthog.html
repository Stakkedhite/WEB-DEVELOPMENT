WARTHOG

Warthogs are sparsely-haired wild pigs found across sub-Saharan Africa.
These unique animals have large heads and bodies with short legs, coarse manes down their backs, and long, thin tails they hold upright when running.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the Warthogs at pen A22, right next to the amphitheatre.
