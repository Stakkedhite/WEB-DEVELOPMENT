ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

Elephants are special due to their remarkable size,
intelligence, complex social structures,and impressive memory.

LIONS

As apex predators, lions play a crucial role in their ecosystems
by regulating prey populations.

MONKEYS

Monkeys are intelligent animals that are good at solving problems.
Almost all types live together in groups.

PANDAS

The panda, with its distinctive black and white coat,
is adored by the world and considered a national treasure in China.

WARTHOG

As grazers, they aerate the soil with their snouts while scavenging
for food, which supports plant growth

GEMSBOK

The most intriguing characteristic of the gemsbok
is his ability to endure and withstand the very hot conditions.

GIRAFFES

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, with males reaching up to 5.5 meters in height.

GORILLAS

Gorillas are large powerful animal with a strong prominent chests and shoulders.

KOALA

The koala, sometimes inaccurately called the koala bear,
is an arboreal herbivorous marsupial native to Australia
