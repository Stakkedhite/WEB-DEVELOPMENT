Who is Black Widow

Black Widow, whose real name is Natasha Romanoff,
is a highly skilled spy and a member of the Avengers in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
She is known for her combat abilities, proficiency with weapons, and espionage background,
having been trained from childhood in the Red Room, a Soviet spy training program.
