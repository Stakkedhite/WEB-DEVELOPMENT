Who is THOR

Thor is the Norse god of thunder, lightning, storms, and protection of humankind. He is a prominent figure
in Germanic paganism and is associated with strength, sacred groves, and fertility. Thor is known for his hammer,
Mjollnir, which he uses to wield lightning and thunder.
